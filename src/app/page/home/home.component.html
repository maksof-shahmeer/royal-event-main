About Royal Events

Royal events were established in 2002 at Tiruvarur, a suburb of Tamilnadu, India named as “chutties events” and started in Melbourne in 2010. The legacy of Royal Events has now been passed on to second generation event planners who have a well established family business in Hyderabad (India) started in early 70's and continue with a passion to serve in event décor field keeping our South East Asian traditions alive.

Here at royal events we offer a service equal to none and pride ourselves on the attention we offer to all our clients whatever the occasion making your event a memory.

Stylish and unique designs, professional service and attention to detail are the key objectives of our company. Those core fundamentals have helped us to grow over the years to be in the industry’s most reputable décor service and mandaps provider. We serve for all events; weddings,birthdays,corporate events and any special occasions…whatever the occasion we offer that little extra special glitz to make your event truly memorable. Our aim as a market leading company to offer you our expertise in creating stunning, innovative designs all at affordable prices.

Make your event one to remember visit us or browse through the website for the Australia’s premiere special events, traditional and latest stage décor service providers."
